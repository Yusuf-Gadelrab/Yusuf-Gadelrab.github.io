What It Does

All 18 pain points of a freight back office. Covered.

FreightDesk covers the full 18-point workload of a small-freight financial controller — end to end. Tap a module to see what's inside.

Billing Ops. Eight billing workflows that normally live in someone's head and a dozen spreadsheet tabs — flagged, reconciled, and drafted automatically.
01Stale bank / payment status flags — catches out-of-date payment info before a check goes to the wrong place.
02False delay flagging — catches the ~40% of "delayed" flags that turn out to be false positives.
03POD verification — confirms proof-of-delivery is actually in hand before billing moves.
04Advance reconciliation — matches advances against settlements and kills double-pay risk.
05W9 tracking — chases missing W9s and catches the ~20% that come back undeliverable.
0630-day past-due tracker — the payment clock, watched automatically.
07Release-list generation — the daily "safe to release" list, built for you.
08Billing dispute reply drafting — the awkward emails, drafted with the right numbers already in them.
AR Ops. Ten accounts-receivable workflows — from invoice queue to ready-for-payment — including the messy per-customer document rules.
09Invoice queue management — every invoice tracked from created to collected.
10Rate reconciliation — fuel-surcharge and accessorial matching against the contracted rate.
11Sales sign-off routing — rate corrections routed to the right salesperson for approval.
12Carrier / vendor verification checks — automated sanity checks before money moves.
13POD prep — single-PDF or separate-page formats, matched to each customer's rules.
14Accessorial tracking — detention, lumper, layover charges chased so they don't leak revenue.
1530-day AR aging follow-ups — polite, persistent, and never forgotten.
16Ready-for-payment queue — a clean list of what's cleared to pay, updated continuously.
17AR reconciliation — remittances matched to invoices without the spreadsheet gymnastics.
18Incoming-message reply automation — every inbound AR email answered correctly (see next tab).
Reply Automation. The engine underneath it all — an AI back-office clerk that never sleeps, plugged straight into your billing / AR / dispatch inbox.
1Reads every incoming email the moment it lands.
2Classifies it — AR reconciliation? POD request? billing dispute? advance question?
3Drafts the correct reply — pulling the right context, numbers, and documents automatically.
4Sends it — fully automatic, or held for one-click approval. Your call, per email type.
